The Quiet Reader

Welcome to the RSVP Pocket E-Reader. This is a sample book, written only so
that you have something to read the very first time you open the app.

Rapid Serial Visual Presentation shows you one word at a time, in the same
place on the screen. Your eyes do not need to move across a line or jump back
to the start of the next one. The words come to you, and you decide how fast
they arrive.

Begin slowly. Two hundred words each minute is a calm, comfortable pace, about
the speed of a person reading aloud. When that feels easy, nudge the speed up a
little. Three hundred, then four hundred. Most readers are surprised by how
quickly a steady rhythm starts to feel natural.

If a sentence ends, the reader rests on its final word a moment longer. A comma
earns a shorter pause. A long word is held a touch more than a short one. These
tiny breaths are what keep fast reading from feeling like a race.

When you want to stop, pause. The word stays on the screen, waiting, asking
nothing of you. When you are ready, begin again. Nothing here connects to a
network. Nothing is counting, scoring, or watching. There is only the next
word, and then the one after that.

Read a single paragraph, or read until the sun goes down. The book remembers
where you were, and so, soon enough, will you. Take a breath, choose your
speed, and let the words come to you, one at a time, all the way to the end.
